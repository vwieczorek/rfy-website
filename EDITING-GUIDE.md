# Website Editing Guide for Melissa

## Logging In

1. Open your web browser
2. Go to: **https://rfy.thewicksproject.org/admin/**
3. Click the **"Login with GitHub"** button
4. Sign in with your GitHub account (first time only)
5. Click **"Authorize"** when prompted

You're now in the Content Manager!

## The Dashboard

After logging in, you'll see a list of content sections on the left:

- **Site Settings** - Organization info, contact details, social media
- **Statistics** - Impact numbers shown on the website
- **People** - Staff and Board member profiles
- **About Page** - History timeline, partners
- **Events Page** - Sponsorship levels
- **Donate Page** - Donation impact descriptions
- **Volunteer Page** - Volunteer opportunities
- **Success Stories** - Testimonials and stories
- **Services** - Mental health and restorative justice info

## Making Changes

### Step 1: Select What to Edit
Click on any section in the left sidebar, then click on the specific item you want to edit.

### Step 2: Edit the Content
- Type in the text fields to change content
- Use the formatting toolbar for bold, italic, links
- Click "Choose an image" to upload or select photos

### Step 3: Save or Publish
- **Save** (top right) = Saves your work but doesn't make it live
- **Publish** (top right) = Makes your changes live on the website

### Step 4: Wait ~30 seconds
After publishing, wait about 30 seconds, then refresh the website to see your changes.

## Common Tasks

### Update Staff Member
1. Click **People** → **Staff Members**
2. Find the staff member in the list
3. Edit their name, title, or photo
4. Click **Publish**

### Add New Staff Member
1. Click **People** → **Staff Members**
2. Scroll to the bottom of the staff list
3. Click **"Add items"**
4. Fill in name, title, upload photo
5. Click **Publish**

### Update Contact Information
1. Click **Site Settings** → **Organization Info**
2. Update phone, email, or address
3. Click **Publish**

### Update Statistics
1. Click **Statistics** → **Impact Statistics**
2. Change the numbers and labels
3. Click **Publish**

### Add a Testimonial
1. Click **Success Stories** → **Testimonials**
2. Click **"Add items"** at the bottom
3. Enter the quote, person's name, and role
4. Click **Publish**

## Tips

- **Preview before publishing**: Use Save to save drafts, then open the website in another tab to check how things look before clicking Publish.

- **Image sizes**: Photos should be under 5MB. JPG or PNG format works best.

- **Undo mistakes**: If you make a mistake, you can always go back and edit again. The previous version is saved in GitHub.

- **Stuck?**: If something isn't working, try logging out and back in, or clearing your browser cache.
